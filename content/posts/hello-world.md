After nearly a decade of writing C and C++ for embedded devices, building Yocto distros, and debugging firmware over UART at odd hours — I figured it's time to start writing some of this down.

This blog is where I'll share what I learn, mostly from real projects:

- **Yocto** — custom distros, BSP layers, KAS builds, OTA updates with RAUC and SWUpdate
- **Zephyr RTOS** — firmware for nRF, ESP32, OpenThread, BLE, MCUboot DFU
- **Embedded Linux** — kernel config, device tree, systemd, security hardening
- **Edge & IoT** — gateways, wireless protocols, containers on embedded, edge AI

One article a week. Straight from the build directory.

Stay tuned.
